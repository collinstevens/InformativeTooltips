Enhances your ability tooltips by displaying every talent in your current build that affects the ability you're hovering over.

## Features

- Talent breakdown on every ability tooltip - Hover over any ability on your action bar and see a list of every talent (selected or not) that modifies it. Each entry shows the full talent description so you can understand exactly how it interacts with the ability.
- Talent source labels - Each talent entry is labeled with where it comes from: your Class tree, your Spec tree, or your Hero talent tree (e.g. "Colossus", "Mountain Thane").
- Active vs inactive talent highlighting - Talents you've selected are displayed in gold, while unselected talents are shown in grey.
- Macro support - Works with macro tooltips too, not just direct ability tooltips.
- Spell replacement awareness - Correctly tracks talents for spells that replace other spells (e.g. Gloomblade replacing Backstab, Drain Soul replacing Shadow Bolt).
- Hide glyph modifiers - Optional setting to remove glyph text from tooltips.
- Shift key toggle - Optional setting to require holding Shift to display talent information.
- Automatic updates - The talent cache refreshes automatically whenever you change your talent build.

## Slash Commands

- `/it settings` - Open the settings panel
